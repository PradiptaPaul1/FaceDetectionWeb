In this current situation the use of face masks appears to be a viable option for restricting the spread of SARS-CoV-2 virus. As this virus spread mainly from person to person through respiratory droplets, it is important to wear a mask to prevent this droplets to travel into the air while coughing, sneezing or talking and infect other people nearby. In this context, our Face M@sk Recognition System is developed to ensure that people's faces are masked correctly in public places where there is a risk of getting infected from the virus, such as in shopping malls, parks, hospitals or where there is a large gathering of people.


The main purpose of our system is to detect peoples who are not wearing face mask in public places, through surveillance camera, so that the officials can take necessary action against them.
